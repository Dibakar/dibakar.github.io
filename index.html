About Me

I am a Senior Research Engineer at Arm Research in Austin, TX.
Before joining Arm in July 2017, I received a Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering specializing in Computer Architecture from University of Wisconsin-Madison in 2017. I was advised by Prof. Mikko Lipasti. I received my Bachelor's Degree (B.E.) in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from Birla Institute of Technology & Science (Pilani), India in 2008 and Master's Degree (M.S.) in Computer Engineering from Texas A&M University in 2011.

Research Interests

Computer Architecture, Machine Learning, Software Engineering, Memory System Design, Non-Volatile Memories
